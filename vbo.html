Världens Västerbottensost®

The cheesemakers behind famous Swedish cheese Västerbottensost ® wanted to let young adults learn about their history and the story behind how the best cheese in Sweden was invented. We found a way to attract more young adults to their website, as well as incorporate more elements of storytelling into it.

Multiple approaches to increase the interest in the story were tested, including gamification and the option to have the story read to you like a podcast. User tests however showed that this alone would be enough for them to go out of their way to visit the website.

We now knew that part of the problem was not a lack of interest in learning about the history, rather that the audience didn’t find a reason to go out of their way to visit the cheese’s website. We had to give them a reason. Our solution to this was a community feature where users could share their favourite recipes with Västerbottensost® from around the world. They could then be given the option to listen to the story while doing something else, for example cooking.

My parts in the project
