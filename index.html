My expertise is making the user feel like an expert

The greatest keys to a good user experience are familiarity and predictability. If the users of a system expect something to happen when they perform an action, that is exactly what should happen.

There is a problem with the statement above, and it is that all users are different. That is where I come in.

As a user-centered designer and developer, currently in my fifth and last year of the Master of Science program in Interaction Technology and Design at Umeå University, I am equpied with the tools and knowledge to understand the target audience and find a solution that satisfies all users.
