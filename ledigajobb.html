What if finding a job didnâ€™t mean hours of editing you resume and writing cover letters? In this project, in collaboration with Swedish tech company Noor, we completely rethink the typical job application process to make applying for a job as easy as possible when using their service ledigajobb.se.

Based on research on the target audience we envisioned a system where the applicant can have a clear overview of the entire job application process and upload required documents only when they are needed, drastically reducing the amount of redundant work for both applicant and employer.

The solution also included a step by step resume-builder and a personalized start page to show more relevant job offers according to the applicants competences.
