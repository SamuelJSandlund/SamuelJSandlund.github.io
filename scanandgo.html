Scan & Go!

Part of a course in Human-Computer Interaction at Ume√• University, we set out to increase the effectiveness of Human-Machine Teaming in self service shopping systems. The target audience for the project was young adults, mainly students and careerists.

We tend to make good teams with those we know well, and what device are we more familiar with today than our phones. Instead of everyone having to learn how different types of hand-scanners or self-checkout desks work at every store we wanted to develop a universal concept where a smart phone can be used as the go to shopping partner. Features include finding what items are in store, scanning QR-codes and handling payments.
